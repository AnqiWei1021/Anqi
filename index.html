Hiiiii, everyone.

I’m Anqi Wei, currently studying MA Digital Arts & Humanities at UCC. My work moves between research, storytelling and digital media, exploring how technology, memory and everyday life collide.

I like working with text, images and small interactive moments, and I’m especially curious about how people feel, respond and reshape meaning in digital spaces.

When I’m not studying, you’ll probably find me walking in the city, taking photos, or collecting tiny fragments of stories.
